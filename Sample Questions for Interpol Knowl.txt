Sample Questions for Interpol Knowledge Graph
🏆 RANK Intent (Most/Top/Which has most)
Countries

Which countries have the most sanctioned individuals?
What are the top 5 countries with sanctioned people?
Which nations have the highest number of sanctions?
Rank countries by number of sanctioned individuals

Organizations

Which organizations have the most sanctioned people?
What are the top companies with sanctioned individuals?
Which organizations employ the most sanctioned people?

Sanction Types

What are the most common types of sanctions?
Which sanction categories are most frequent?


🔢 COUNT Intent (How many/Total)
General Counts

How many people are sanctioned?
How many sanctioned individuals are there in total?
What is the total count of sanctions?

Specific Counts

How many people are sanctioned from Russia?
How many organizations are in the database?
How many different types of sanctions exist?
How many countries have sanctioned individuals?


📋 LIST Intent (List/Show all)
Entities

List all sanction types

List all organizations in the database
What are the different sanction categories?

Filtered Lists

List all people sanctioned for terrorism
Show all Russian nationals who are sanctioned
List organizations from the United States

People

Organizations

Find organizations from China
Show me companies that employ sanctioned individuals
Which organizations are linked to terrorism sanctions?

Combined Queries

🎯 Complex/Advanced Questions
Multi-relationship

Trend Questions (if you have date data)
